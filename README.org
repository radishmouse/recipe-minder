* Recipe Minder

"That app my wife has wanted me to make for keeping track of when we make which recipes."

** Data Model

*** Tags

- name

*** Recipes

- name
- number_of_servings
- tag_id


*** Ingredients

- name

*** Amounts

- recipe_id
- ingredient_id
- amount_per_serving

*** Meals

- made_on
- notes

*** Recipes_Meals

- recipe_id
- meal_id

*** Steps

- step_number
- description
- version_number
- recipe_id

** Queries

*** DONE we have broccoli, what can we make?

*** DONE what haven't we made in a while?
*** DONE I need to eat something quickly. What's easy? (This is a tag search)
